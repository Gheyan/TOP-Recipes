Tempura Batter

Description

  The recipe is very good 10/10

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cold water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat egg whites in a bowl until frothy.
  3. Fold flour and cold water into egg whites until the batter is barely mixed.
  4. Coat vegetables or meat with cornstarch, then dip into the tempura batter and fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
