Tempura Batter

Description

  The recipe is very good 10/10

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions
  • Fresh ginger
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. Soak the cedar planks.
  2. Make the marinade.
  3. Grill the planks until they start to smoke and crackle.
  4. Place the salmon on the planks, close the grill, and grill until the salmon is flaky.
